What is clip.cool?

A GIF repository where you make the GIFs. Clip a moment from a video on any other site and it becomes a tidy, searchable, shareable looping clip — no plugin, no download, no GIF-maker app.

Turn any video into a clip

See a moment you want — a stream, a movie scene, a game, a clip on another site? Open Create, share the browser tab you're watching it in, play the moment, and hit Record. Drag to crop, trim the ends, add a caption, and publish. We do the rest.

Real video, not a 1987 GIF

Old GIFs are huge and look bad. Behind the scenes every clip is transcoded into a modern looping video that loads fast and looks sharp, while still giving you a real .gif to drop into chats that only autoplay GIFs (Discord, Signal, and friends).

Search and remix

Clips are indexed by what's in them — the text on screen and what's said — so you can search for the one you remember, or just browse what's new. Like one? Remix it: re-trim, re-crop, and re-caption any clip into a new one of your own.
